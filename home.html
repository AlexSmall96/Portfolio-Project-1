About

Philosophy

Alex's Restaurant is a true reflection of our passions for exquisite fine dining cusine, and veganism. We believe in showing the very best of what plant based food can offer, creating fully vegan versions of classical style dishes.

Food

All dishes are prepared freshly on site. We frequently forage locally for herbs, fruit and wild mushrooms, and believe in allowing the natural flavour of plants to shine.

The Restaurant

Our restaurant has a friendly, welcoming atmosphere, where guests can relax and expect a highly professional service. We are happy to accomadate any dietary requests.
